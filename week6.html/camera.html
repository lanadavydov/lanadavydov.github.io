CAMERA

the camera lense and it's duality between perception and reality.

it's all about perception...

ordinary objects in reality can range from keys, to a phone, to a wallet. ... yet in the warzone, those objects can be anything from an array of varying weapons. it is suggested that the perceptions of reality is shaped by language and propaganda, as the poem uses phrases such as "collateral damage" and "precision bombing" to justify violent acts.
